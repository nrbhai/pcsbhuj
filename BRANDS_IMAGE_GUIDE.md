## Design Guidelines

### Category Images
- **Resolution**: Minimum 1200x600px
- **Format**: JPG (optimized)
- **Style**: Professional product photography
- **Brightness**: Well-lit, clear visibility
- **Focus**: Show multiple products or installation scenes

### Brand Logos
- **Format**: PNG with transparent background
- **Resolution**: 200x100px (or higher with same ratio)
- **Style**: Official brand logos
- **Color**: Full color (not grayscale)
- **Padding**: Include some white space around logo

## Optimization Tips
- Compress JPG images to ~200KB max
- Use PNG-8 for simple logos, PNG-24 for complex ones
- Ensure logos are high quality but optimized file size
- Test on white and colored backgrounds

## Logo Sources
Download official brand logos from:
- Company official press/media kits
- brandsoftheworld.com
- seeklogo.com
- wikimedia commons (for open source logos)

**Important**: Use only official, authorized brand logos. Ensure you have proper rights to use them.
